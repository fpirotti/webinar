Time-series of RADAR backscatter using GEE and R

Francesco Pirotti <francesco.pirotti@unipd.it>

#Summer Webinar Series — August 5, 2020

Agenda

  • Load to Google Earth areas which have been subject to changes in the past 5 years
    • I will use areas strongly damaged by VAIA but you can use your own
    • The data is also downloadable from the windthrow database that was created for the paper by [@Forzieri2020] linking to an open database containing polygons of damaged areas.
  • Extract time series of Sentinel 1 backscatter data
  • Import extracted data in R CRAN
  • Plot time series data and applying a Z-score filter over past N values to detect changes

Before we begin

Where is the webinar?

Seminars are open (moderated) and accessible via zoom @ https://unipd.zoom.us/j/98756930077

What software should I have?

For this Webinar we use Google Earth Engine (GEE) and RStudio - if you want to follow along, make sure you have an account with GEE and have installed R + RStudio in your PC - and you have downloaded some polygons of data - I suggest to start with not more than 10-20 polygons for the excercises.
